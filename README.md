# Third-Party Risk Management & Vendor Governance Program

Comprehensive TPRM framework for federal contractors and enterprises managing vendor risk across CUI handling environments.

---

## 4 Phases

**Phase 1: Foundation & Vendor Governance Framework**  
3-layer governance structure, vendor criticality assessment (3-dimensional matrix), risk scoring methodology (Likelihood × Impact × Regulatory Exposure), vendor categorization framework, compliance mapping, policy framework

**Phase 2: Vendor Inventory & Criticality Assessment**  
Vendor discovery (7 sources), master data collection, multi-dimensional criticality scoring (1.0-3.0 scale), vendor portfolio analysis, assessment prioritization roadmap, executive dashboards

**Phase 3: Vendor Assessment & Risk Scoring**  
Risk-based assessment procedures (CRITICAL/HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW), security questionnaires, evidence collection & review, assessment reporting, control assessment templates, remediation tracking, compliance mapping

**Phase 4: Vendor Monitoring & Ongoing Oversight**  
Continuous monitoring (quarterly through biennial by criticality), automated vulnerability scanning, breach database monitoring, certification tracking, performance metrics, escalation procedures (3 levels), vendor relationship management, executive reporting
